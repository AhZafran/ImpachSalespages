## Brand Guidelines

**Colors:**
- Primary: #073050 (Dark Blue)

- Green (Savings): #16A34A
- Background: White/Light Gray

**Typography:**
- Headings: Bold, large, attention-grabbing
- Body: Minimum 16px, good line height
- Suggested fonts: Inter/Poppins (headings), Open Sans (body)
